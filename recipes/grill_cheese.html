Classic Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Description

A classic grilled cheese sandwich is a simple yet delicious comfort food made with bread and melted cheese. It's quick to make and perfect for lunch or a light dinner.

Ingredients:

  • 4 slices of bread
  • 4 slices of cheddar cheese (or any cheese of your choice)
  • 2 tablespoons butter, softened

Steps:

  1. Heat a skillet over medium heat.
  2. Butter one side of each slice of bread.
  3. Place two slices of bread, butter side down, in the skillet.
  4. Place two slices of cheese on each piece of bread in the skillet.
  5. Top each with another slice of bread, butter side up.
  6. Cook until the bread is golden brown and the cheese is melted, about 2-3 minutes per side.
  7. Remove from the skillet, cut in half, and serve immediately.
